GAMEPLAY RULES

Make sure you uphold the game rules, break any rule and you will be banned!

  • No hacking the game, this includes modified controllers, aim bots, etc. We are here to play for reals not cheat.
  • Sportsmanship is of the utmost importance, we are all here to have fun and enjoy Halo, do not ruin it for others.
  • Inappropriate language is not tolerated! this includes what is said in chat, and what is said to other participants outside of the game.
  • If you signed up and are scheduled to play in a Tournament you must show.
  • You are the only person allowed to use your profile, its cheating to allow someone else to play for you.
